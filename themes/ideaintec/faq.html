Information IIT and objective

1.What is the main objective of IIT?

The main objective of IIT is to empower the community for providing excellent standard of IT training and to ensure the quality of delivery for the long run. We help to build your career and to assist you towards entrepreneurship.

And we have one online platform for IT Training that is called Creative-E-School. ♦ For Chattogram the official page is- https://www.facebook.com/IIT.Chattogram/ ♦ For Khulna the official page is- https://www.facebook.com/IIT.Khulna/ ♦ For E-School the official page is- https://www.facebook.com/CreativeEschool/

3.What is the establishment year of IIT?

We are serving & providing quality training for more than 11 years. The year of establishment is 2008. The institute is dedicated to serving the quality training programs under ISO 9001: 2015 certification which remarks us in the IT world.

4.What is your office address?

Our office is located in Mirza Ruhul Amin Road,(Near to B.N Mosque), Thakurgaon Sadar, Thakurgaon.

5.Where should I contact for admission?

For any type of query related with admission please contact with Mr. Rannojit Sarkar, Head of Admission at 01617047345

6.What are the pre-requirements for enrolling in your institute?

The Documents that you need to submit before enrolling are- One copy passport size photo, Photocopy of your NID or Birth Certificate, Certificate of any one of your Academic certificate (S.S.C / H.S.C)

7.Where can I find the course materials?

You will be able to collect all our course materials from our premises. You can directly come and have a discussion with our counselling team. They will provide you with all the necessary documents.

8.Does IIT offer any intership facility for its studends?

Yes, we offer internship facilities for the students. Students have to apply for the Internship Facilities to the Director through the respective program coordinator.

Information freelancing

1.Do you have any freelancing course?

With every course offered we provide techniques and show all the possible ways to become a freelancer. We help you to work and generate income as a freelancer. You will be able to know the ways to promote your works in online marketplaces.

Information fee and payment

1.How much is the course fee?

The course fee for different courses vary. You can go to our list of courses here, pick a course that you prefer and see for the course fee.

2.Can I pay the course fee by installments?

You can pay the course fee in three installments maximum after enrolling in the course. Besides we offer discounts at different times that will deduct the course fee according to its percentage

3.What type of payments are accepted?

We accept Visa, American Express and Master Card. We also accept payment by Bkash.

4.Why the course fee is so high?

We believe in quality of learning. The standard of learning that we offer is worth what you will pay.

5.Do you give money refund?

No. After enrollment we don't allow money refund even if you want to cancel your admission. But if for any reason your class doesn't start within 2 months of the admission date then after 2 months company will refund your money. We will refund the full amount except the admission fee that is BDT 1000.

6.What is minimum required down payment?

The minimum required down payment for any course is TK 8,500/- and rest of the amount can be paid in installments.

7.Can I pay my course fee in following 5 months or doing a job in IIT?

Sorry sir, you can not provide your payments in such a way, you have to pay your admission fee and course fee in miximum 4 installments.First of all you have pay 7000 taka(1000 add fee +6000 course fee) for admission purpose and last payment would be maximum 4000 taka between these you have to pay your remaining fees.

8.Will there be any discount for the next month or following months?

Now ,we have a 25% discount but what would be the following discount i do not know yet.It would be decided in the following month.you just follow our facebook page or website for getting updated information.
